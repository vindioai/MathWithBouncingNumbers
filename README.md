# Math With Bouncing Numbers 

**Privacy Policy & Disclaimer**

## Privacy Policy
**Effective Date:** 23/01/2026

Gluten Free Smart Food Scanner respects your privacy. This document explains how the app handles data and permissions.

### 1. Camera Access
- The app requires access to your device camera to scan barcodes on food products.
- Images are processed locally on your device.
- No images are stored or transmitted to any server.

### 2. Internet Access
- The app uses the internet to retrieve product, barcode, and ingredient information from the Open Food Facts public database.
- No personal user data is transmitted by the app.

### 3. Ads & Monetization
- The app uses **Google AdMob** to display advertisements.
- AdMob may collect device identifiers (such as Advertising ID) to provide personalized or non-personalized ads.
- The app itself does **not** collect, store, or share any personal information.

### 4. User Rights (GDPR)
- If you are located in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland, you have rights under the GDPR.
- You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data.
- You can withdraw your consent at any time via: Settings → Privacy → Manage Ad Preferences.
- You have the right to object to the processing of your personal data
- You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

### 5. No User Accounts or Personal Data
- No user login or registration is required.
- The app does not record, store, or share personal user data.

### 6. Third-Party Services
- **Open Food Facts** provides product and ingredient information from a public database.
- **Google AdMob** may process limited device information in accordance with its own privacy policies.

### 7. Contact Information
For questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact:  
**Email:** info@vindioai.com

## Disclaimer
- Gluten Free Smart Food Scanner provides ingredient and allergen information for **informational and educational purposes only**.
- The app does **not** provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
- Ingredient and allergen information may change over time; users should always verify product labels.
- The app relies on third-party data sources, primarily **Open Food Facts**, and accuracy cannot be guaranteed.
- Advertisements displayed via Google AdMob do not collect personal user data beyond device identifiers used for ad personalization.
